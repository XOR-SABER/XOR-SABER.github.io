COMPUTER ORG:

://Course description.

A pleasant ride through ARM32 Assembly assembly, and C++ programing,

This course introduces functional organization of digital computers and low level programming; internal representation of data, binary arithmetic, machine instructions, addressing modes, subroutine linkage, macros and interrupts, Assembly language programming.
